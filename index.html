Dice Game Instructions

Objective:

  • Be the first player to reach a total score of 50.

    • Players:

  • 2

    • Gameplay:

  • Players take turns rolling a 6-sided die.
  • Each roll adds to the player's "current score" for that turn.
  • Players can roll the die multiple times per turn.
  • Rolling a 1 ends the turn immediately without adding to the overall score.
  • At any time during their turn, players can choose to "hold" their current score.
  • Holding adds the current score to the playerâ€™s overall total and ends their turn.
  • The game continues until one player reaches 50 points.
